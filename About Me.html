Get to know me...

     Hello! My name is Erikson Myles Jardim, author and creator of the many books that you will find on this website and scattered throughout Fimfiction. I live as most of you probably know in Canada, Ontario and I have lived here for all of my life. I will say however, that I do visit my family oversees in Finland, I always find myself looking forwards to each trip as it's an experience that is always different. This being said I'm a very adventurous person, most time I find myself completely lost.

    I started writing on Fimfiction on May,1st,2014. As some of you may know that is the exact date that 'Falling For a Star' came out. What drove me to write a story was that I simply ran out of fics that I 'truly' loved. I know that there are millions of amazing books on that site and hundreds more arrive everyday, however I was not satisfied (call me selfish I know). So I opened up my word document and started my very own story on which I and the community could alter at there own will.

    Now to say that the book did well when it first came out is a misinterpretation that I find most people make. My book 'Falling For a Star' actually started off negative and then worked its way up to 50-50 and finally to what it is now over 4 times more likes. This just proves my point that if you start a story, keep working on it and editing it you'll succeed. Now that I think about it people have been telling me that all the time "aim for the stars and you'll get there.".

    Anyway... I myself do not and will not ever ask people that read my stories for money. This is a hobby of mine just like coding is and it's something that I would feel guilty if I were to ask for money. Mostly because everyone on Fimfiction already gives away their books for free. It was them that inspired me to make my own novel, a feeling I hope to pass onto you.

    People say that I have a very happy and positive attitude, do I believe any of their talk; no not really. While I do keep a positive vibe around other people I find myself very stressed out while I'm alone. This was another reason I started writing, it let me express myself non-directly. This open vent vastly improved my positive aurora that a lot of people commented on. I started to make many more friends, not to mention the amount of more parties I went to.

    What I find most people asking me about is if I watch the show. My simple answerer to that is not any more, I used to watch the show back when I was starting 'Falling For a Star'. After the fourth season I stopped watching any and all episodes. I have not watched any of the movies either, as you will see my writing will tend to not include any of the later episodes or movies.

    For you that really want to know, my favourite pone is Spitfire, Luna coming close but not quite reaching top tier. Out of the mane six I find Rainbow Dash to be most like me, bash, loyal, egotistic (sadly) and the undeniable need for speed.

    As of right now I'm working for a courir company and that is how I am able to pay the bills and live. This however is a temporary job as I am attending a collage for aviation. After that I will be moving on to become a pilot, flying oil and supplies up north. That really is all I have to say about my life.

    If you ever wish to contact me, you can skype me at Erikson1231 or email me at fim.ccpone@gmail.com.
